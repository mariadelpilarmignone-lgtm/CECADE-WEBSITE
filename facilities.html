facilities

Cecade has many facilities for both horses and riders, making your experience much more comfortable. You can also enjoy different activities every weekend, such as federation competitions, farm visits, workshops, walks through the orchard, and more.

CECADE counts with 68 boxes for horses. The boxes are divided in 4 blocks, with 17 boxes in each. Also by the begining of 2026 the new prenium boxes would be avaliable to buy.

CECADE counts with 55 corrals for horses, all around the property. the horses can interactuate with their friends and have a space to relax from all the practice they had.

Apart from corrals and boxes, CECADE counts with other facilities such as an electric walker, horse-leaping field, school track and a green track for competitions.
